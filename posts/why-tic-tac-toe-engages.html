Start Generous

Let players win early to build confidence and learn the physics.

Ramp Gently

Increase AI speed and prediction accuracy gradually as the player's score increases.

Telegraph Strategy

The AI's movements and paddle angle should give subtle clues to its next shot, allowing players to learn, not just react.
